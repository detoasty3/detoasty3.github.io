    GAMC

    The GAMC (Geometry AMC) is a mock AMC consisting of exclusively geometry problems. The difficulty of the contest is somewhere between AMC 10 and AMC 12 (i.e. an AMC 11). However, unlike the DMC 11, certain AMC 12 concepts are used on the GAMC. This contest was written by depsilon0 and DeToasty3 and test-solved by AOPSmathematics, ApraTrip, and Radio2.
